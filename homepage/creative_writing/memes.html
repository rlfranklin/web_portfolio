Write.

Everyone can write.

Not everyone writes well.

The more you write, the better you'll get.

The more you read, the better you'll get.

Meme 1

I found this one a few years ago...I can't remember where though.

1. Tell us about your favorite writing project/universe that you’ve worked with and why.

2. How many characters do you have? Do you prefer males or females?

3. How do you come up with names, for characters (and for places if you’re writing about fictional places)?

4. Tell us about one of your first stories/characters!

5. Where are you most comfortable writing? At what time of day? Computer or good ol’ pen and paper?

6. Do you listen to music while you write? What kind? Are there any songs you like to relate/apply to your characters?

7. What’s your favorite genre to write? To read?

8. How do you get ideas for your characters? Describe the process of creating them.

9. What are some really weird situations your characters have been in?

10. Who is your favorite character to write? Least favorite

11. In what story did you feel you did the best job of world building?

12. What’s your favorite culture to write, fictional or not?

13. How do you map out locations, if needed? Do you have any to show us?

14. Mid way question! Tell us about a writer you admire, whether professional or not!

15. Do you write romantic relationships? How do you do with those, and how “far” are you willing to go in your writing?

16. Favorite antagonist and why!

17. Favorite minor that decided to shove him self into the spotlight and why?
18. What are your favorite character interactions to write?

19. How long does it usually take you to complete an entire story—from planning to writing to posting (if you post your work)?

20. How willing are you to kill your characters if the plot so demands it? What’s the most interesting way you’ve killed someone?

21. Do any of your characters have pets? Tell us about them.

22. Let’s talk art! Do you draw your characters? Do others draw them?

23. Along similar lines, do appearances play a big role in your stories? Tell us about them, or if not, how you go about designing your characters.

24. Have you ever written a character with physical or mental disabilities? Describe them, and if there’s nothing major to speak of, tell us a few smaller ones.

 

 

Meme 2

1. How often do you create new characters?

2. Have you ever written a protagonist who is the opposite sex? Different ethnicity?

3. Is there a genre you generally try to avoid?

4. Do you carry around a notebook and pen?

5. How many journals do you currently have?

6. Have many journals have you filled?

7. Where and when do you often find yourself inspired to write?

8. When do you generally get ideas?

9. What's the longest piece you have written?

10. What is the shortest?
