>What are Emails and How to Send One

Emails are a widely used and established method of online communication. They remain one of the fastest ways to deliver text, images, and small files. By the end of this tutorial, you'll learn what emails are and how to send one.

What is an Email

Email, short for "electronic mail," is a digital message online users send to each other. To send and receive these messages, you'll need an email address, which is your electronic mailbox where messages are delivered, and an internet connection. When you create your email address, an email service provider like Gmail will assign it to you if it’s available. All email addresses must be unique. You’ll also need an email address for your recipient. In this guide, we'll use SampleEmailAddress@gmail.com as our example.

Note: To access Gmail and create a new email, you'll need to sign up for a Google Account.

How to Send an Email

Once you set up your Google Account to use Gmail, you can send your first email. These next few steps will walk you through the process:

  1. Go to mail.google.com on your web browser to access your Gmail account
  2. Click the Compose button in the top left corner to start a new message

    3. You’ll see the compose panel appear on the bottom right corner of the screen

  3. Add your recipient’s email address to the To: field.
  4. (Optional) Add a Subject line to title your message
  5. Write your message in the compose panel
  6. Click Send to deliver your email to your recipient(s)

Email Delivery and Retraction Window

When you click Send, Gmail offers you five seconds to retract your message before your email will be delivered. Be sure to include all the relevant information, proofread your text, and check that you’re sending your email to the right address. Once your email is delivered, there’s no taking it back. If you forgot to add any information to your initial message, you’ll have to send a follow-up email.

Scheduling Your Email

You also have the option to send your email at a scheduled time.

  • Click the small down arrow on the Send button.

    • A pop-up window will appear with a few optional times

  • Choose a time you’d like to send your email or click Pick date & time for more options
