City

Bright
Never sleeps
Slowing screeches with
Dark tunnels and
Two white lights
Honking hornRushing to get
Nowhere
Lights put on a show
Culture flourishes with music
And dance
And costumes
Window shoppers
Crowding sidewalks
Blinding flashes
Famous statues
Buildings reaching
Toward clouds
Languages jumbled
Into one
Nice dresses
Cute shirts
Fancy suits
Weird shoes
A life of it’s own
A place of surprises
The stories are different
And will never end
But not everyone can handle it…

Time Flies


Minute
hand takes
it’s dear
sweet time
reaching
the ebony
strokes that
read “12.”
Ticking
tocking
seconds
slide
smoothly
south
west
and finally
north.
It
cannot
move
any faster
This timeless
torture,
nowhere near
ending.


Battery

Shattering
Splattering
Chattering
Choosing
Oozing
Confusing
Losing
Boozing
